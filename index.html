Why Visit Albania?

Albania is one of the oldest and most culturaly rich countries in Europe. It has some of the most beautiful natural landscapes, a delicious cuisine and it's very affordable. Albania touches both the Adriatic and Ionian Seas, with clear waters. With a language that is not related or similar to any other in the world, Albania is one of the oldest country in Europe.
